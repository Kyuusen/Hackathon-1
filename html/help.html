Healing process

The tattoo healing process usually takes 2-4 weeks. Following these simple rules will help ensure a smoother healing process and a beautiful, long-lasting tattoo!

Do's

Don't
Do clean the tattoo with mild soap and lukewarm water to remove any excess ointment, blood, or plasma. Don’t pick, scratch, or peel at any scabs or flakes that form—this can cause scarring and damage the tattoo.
Do apply a thin layer of healing ointment or fragrance-free lotion to keep the tattoo moisturized. Don’t soak the tattoo in water (no swimming pools, hot tubs, or baths) until it's fully healed.
Do wear loose clothing to avoid friction or irritation on the tattooed area Don’t expose the tattoo to direct sunlight or tanning beds, as UV rays can fade the ink.
Do keep the tattoo out of direct sunlight to prevent fading during the healing process. Don’t apply thick ointments or petroleum jelly that could clog pores and slow the healing.
Do get plenty of rest and stay hydrated to support your body’s healing process. Don’t wear tight or restrictive clothing over the tattoo that could rub or irritate the area.
